MY STORY

  • I was born in San Francisco, California on September 21, 2008, and moved to London, Canada when I was two. My journey in golf began at the age of seven when my sports-loving dad introduced me to the game. Since then, I have been fortunate to receive unwavering support from my loving family, including my dedicated mother, who provides me with the resources I need, and my younger brother Jayden, who, while more interested in video games, is always cheering me on.
  • Since I was ten, I have been training under Gareth Raflewski, who is not just my swing and short game coach but also a mentor who emphasizes the importance of being a good person as well as a good player.
  • Golf is my passion, and I truly cherish every moment on the course. Whether it's practicing my swing, refining my short game, or simply enjoying the fresh air, I find immense joy in the sport. Now at 15, I aspire to play college golf and eventually become a professional golfer. My dreams include winning the US Women's Open, earning the Rookie of the Year title, and becoming the best golfer in the world. I understand that this journey will be long and challenging, but I believe in myself and my dreams, and I am committed to pursuing them relentlessly.
  • I would be honored to have your support and guidance on this journey. Will
