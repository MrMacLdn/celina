MY STORY

  • I was born in San Francisco on September 21, 2008, and moved to London, Canada when I was 2. At 7, I was out on the range with my dad who loves sports and encountered golf, and since then he has given me a lot of support so I can focus on golf. I also have a dedicated mother who always supports me no matter what I do. I have a younger brother named Jayden, he is 11 years old and loves to play video games rather than golf.
  • My swing, short game and putting Coach is Gareth Raflewski, I have been working with him since I was 10. He is not just my coach, but a life teacher who always tells me to be a good person before becoming a good player.
  • I'm 15, I want to play college golf and become a professional golfer. I dream of winning the US Women's Open, winning the Rookie of the Year, and becoming the best golfer in the world. I know this journey will be long and tough, however, I believe in myself, believe in my dream, and I will do my best without stopping to pursue it. Would you be with me?
