My Story

I was born in California in 2008 maybe? and have been golfing for as long as I can remember. I was introduced to the game by my wonderful father
who continues to motivate me to be my best today along with my super supportive mother. I also have a younger brother who looks up to me
and thinks I am the greatest golfer alive, he's a big source of inspiration as well.  I am the greatest golfer my school and teacher Mr. McIntosh have even seen and I enjoy beating him on the course,
he's very humbled every time I crush him but he can still out drive me.
I have three goals in life: To reach world number 1, win a major championship, and out drive Mr. McIntosh.
Two of those seem within my reach but all three will continue to motivate me as I pursue my destiny of playing pro.  There is more to my story but lets focus more on this teacher of mine... on second thought that's enough about him.
Thank you for listening and following my career.
